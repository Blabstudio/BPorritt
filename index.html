About

Ben Porritt

Founder & President
BPI Public Strategy

Ben Porritt is the founder and president of BPI Public Strategy, a firm representing some of the most recognized brands and leaders in the public and private sector.

With more than two decades of experience spanning media, finance, politics, and sports, Porritt has built winning campaigns, successfully positioned high-profile executives, and managed some of the nation’s most consequential media crises. He currently advises recognizable CEOs, sports team owners, and senior leaders navigating complex public environments.

Prior to launching BPI, Porritt served as an executive for a private equity firm where he oversaw government and public affairs. During his tenure he played a key role in positioning and driving support for more than $30 billion in assets in various industries including sports, energy and transportation — including for publicly traded companies.

A veteran of the White House press office and three presidential campaigns, Porritt began his career leading communications and political strategy for notable elected officials and presidential candidates — experience that continues to inform his approach to high-stakes counsel today.

Porritt holds a Master of Business Administration from the University of Southern California and a Bachelor of Science from Bradley University.
